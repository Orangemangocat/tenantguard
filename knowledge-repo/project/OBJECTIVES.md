# TenantGuard Project Objectives

## Primary Objectives

### 1. Empower Tenants

**Goal:** Provide tenants with accessible, affordable legal representation for landlord-tenant disputes.

**Success Metrics:**
- 60% cost reduction compared to traditional legal services
- High tenant satisfaction scores
- Increased access to legal services for underserved populations

**Current Status:** Foundation laid with case intake form; awaiting full implementation of matching and case management.

---

### 2. Streamline Attorney Workflows

**Goal:** Reduce the time and effort attorneys spend on case intake and management.

**Success Metrics:**
- 70% reduction in case setup time (target: under 1 hour)
- High attorney satisfaction and retention
- Increased attorney efficiency and caseload capacity

**Current Status:** Attorney intake form implemented; dashboard and case management tools pending.

---

### 3. Build a Scalable Platform

**Goal:** Create a platform that can grow to serve more users and expand to new jurisdictions.

**Success Metrics:**
- Platform can handle 1000+ concurrent users
- Easy to add new features and jurisdictions
- Reliable uptime and performance

**Current Status:** SQLite database sufficient for current scale; migration to PostgreSQL planned for future growth.

---

## Secondary Objectives

**Goal:** Protect user data and comply with all relevant regulations.

- High usability scores

**Goal:** Generate revenue to sustain and grow the platform.

**Success Metrics:**

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### User Metrics

- **Number of Tenants:** Total tenants who have submitted cases
- **Number of Attorneys:** Total attorneys on the platform
- **Active Cases:** Number of cases currently in progress
- **Case Resolution Rate:** Percentage of cases successfully resolved

### Efficiency Metrics

- **Time to Match:** Average time from case submission to attorney assignment
- **Case Setup Time:** Average time attorneys spend on initial case setup
- **Platform Uptime:** Percentage of time the platform is available

### Financial Metrics

- **Revenue:** Total revenue generated
- **Cost per Acquisition:** Cost to acquire a new user (tenant or attorney)
- **Customer Lifetime Value:** Average revenue per user over their lifetime

### Satisfaction Metrics

- **Tenant Satisfaction:** Average satisfaction score from tenants
- **Attorney Satisfaction:** Average satisfaction score from attorneys
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Likelihood of users to recommend the platform

---

## Alignment with Mission

All objectives are designed to support the core mission of empowering tenants and providing accessible legal representation. The platform's success will be measured not just by financial metrics, but by the positive impact it has on tenants' lives and the legal system as a whole.
