# TenantGuard Project Summary

## Overview

TenantGuard is a legal-tech platform designed to connect tenants in Davidson County, Tennessee with qualified attorneys for landlord-tenant disputes. The platform streamlines the case intake process for tenants and provides attorneys with tools to efficiently manage their caseload.

## Mission

Empower tenants facing disputes with their landlords by providing accessible, affordable legal representation through technology.

## Target Users

**Primary Users:**
- **Tenants:** Individuals renting properties who need legal assistance with landlord disputes
- **Attorneys:** Licensed legal professionals specializing in landlord-tenant law

**Geographic Scope:**
- Davidson County, Tennessee (with plans to expand)

## Value Proposition

**For Tenants:**
- 60% cost reduction compared to traditional legal services
- Easy-to-use case intake process
- Access to qualified attorneys
- Transparent case tracking

**For Attorneys:**
- 70% reduction in case setup time (from 4.5 hours to under 1 hour)
- Streamlined client intake
- Efficient case management tools
- Flexible budget options for lead generation

## Current Status

**Phase:** Active Development and Testing

**Deployment:** Live at https://www.tenantguard.net

**Repository:** https://github.com/Orangemangocat/tenantguard

## Key Features

1. **Tenant Case Intake:** 8-step guided form with document upload
2. **Attorney Application:** 7-step professional intake with budget configuration
3. **Contact Form:** Professional contact modal with email functionality
4. **Theme System:** 4 customizable color schemes for personalization
5. **Responsive Design:** Mobile-friendly interface

## Technology

**Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn/UI

**Backend:** Python Flask, SQLAlchemy, SQLite

**Infrastructure:** Nginx, Systemd, deployed on testing server
